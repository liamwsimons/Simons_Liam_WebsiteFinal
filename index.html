ABOUT US

We are a small town coffee shop born here in Durango Colorado. We want to create a community oriented space that elevates conversation and community. Inspired by the European model, we want to highlight the beauty of a conversation held over a cup of coffee.

Menu Items

Croissants: Available freshly made every single morning, with classic, chocolate and pistacio.
